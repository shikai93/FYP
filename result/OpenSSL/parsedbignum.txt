Confirm that a^b mod c agrees when calculated cleverly vs naively, for random a, b and c.

Divide the input into three parts, using the values of the first two bytes to choose lengths, which generate b1, b2 and b3. Use three bits of the third byte to choose signs for the three numbers.

mod 0 is undefined
