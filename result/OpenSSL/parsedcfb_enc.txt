The input and output are loaded in multiples of 8 bits. What this means is that if you hame numbits=12 and length=2 the first 12 bits will be retrieved from the first byte and half the second. The second 12 bits will come from the 3rd and half the 4th byte.

Until Aug 1 2003 this function did not correctly implement CFB-r, so it will not be compatible with any encryption prior to that date. Ben.
